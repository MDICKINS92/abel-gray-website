ECL Mews, Bedfordshire

Seven homes: six new-build mews dwellings and one conversion in a conservation area setting

Upcoming Development

About This Development

ECL Mews represents a carefully considered conversion and new build development, following the demolition of the existing office building and part conversion of an adjacent office building into residential use.

The scheme comprises 6 new build mews-style dwellings alongside 1 converted residential dwelling, complemented by high quality landscaping, generous amenity spaces, and thoughtful car parking provision.

Specification & Design

Dwelling Type

2.5 storey mews style dwellings with 2 bedrooms

Layout

Ground floor: 2nd bedroom, bathroom, storage
First floor: Open plan living, dining, kitchen, private balcony (5sqm)
Second floor: Main bedroom with en-suite, rooflights

Materials

Red facing brickwork with red brick detailing, natural slate effect roof tiles, dark UPVC windows, black UPVC rainwater goods, conservation rooflights

Balconies

Glazed balustrades with grey brick stand bond detailing, 5sqm private balcony per dwelling

Amenity & Landscaping

Each dwelling benefits from a private balcony area of 5 square metres. In addition, shared amenity space is proposed to the front of the dwellings, providing an attractive and useable outdoor area for residents with benches and quality planting.

A comprehensive landscaping scheme is proposed, with 13 new trees to be planted alongside hedgerows and shrubbery, ensuring the development integrates sensitively into its surroundings.
