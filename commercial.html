What We Invest In

We invest in a wide range of commercial property assets across London and surrounding areas, focusing on properties with strong fundamentals, sustainable income, and long-term value. Our commercial investment strategy is acquisition-led, targeting well-located assets within established markets.

Abel Gray holds and continues to grow a substantial commercial portfolio across multiple sectors. We actively acquire income-producing properties, owner-occupied buildings, redevelopment opportunities, and value-add investments. All acquisitions are made for long-term ownership and portfolio growth.

Frequently Asked Questions About Selling Commercial Property

What types of commercial property do you buy?

We buy a wide range of commercial property including retail units, offices, industrial premises, restaurants, bars, healthcare facilities, leisure properties, and specialist assets such as petrol stations and data centres.

Do you buy commercial property with tenants in place?

Yes. We actively acquire income-producing commercial investments with existing tenants. We also buy vacant and owner-occupied properties.

How quickly can you complete a commercial property purchase?

As direct buyers using our own capital, we can move quickly and decisively. Timescales depend on the complexity of the transaction, but we aim to complete as efficiently as possible.

Are there any fees to sell my commercial property to you?

No. We are direct buyers. There are no agents' fees, no hidden costs, and no obligations. We handle the entire process ourselves.

What areas do you invest in?

We invest across London, the Midlands and South East England, including Buckinghamshire, Bedfordshire, Northamptonshire, Oxfordshire, Warwickshire, Leicestershire, and the West Midlands.

Do you buy commercial property that needs refurbishment?

Yes. We welcome properties with repositioning or value-add potential. Whether your property needs refurbishment, change of use, or redevelopment, we would be interested to hear from you.
