Abel Gray actively buys land for residential development across Northamptonshire. Whether you own agricultural land, farmland, a paddock, orchard, garden land, a brownfield site, an infill plot, a building plot, or a barn or farm building, we would be interested to hear from you.

Frequently Asked Questions: Selling Land in Northamptonshire

Do you buy land without planning permission in Northamptonshire?

Yes. We buy land with or without planning permission in Northamptonshire. We assess every site on its development potential and manage the entire planning process ourselves at our own risk and cost.

What types of land do you buy in Northamptonshire?

We buy agricultural land, paddocks, orchards, garden land, brownfield sites, infill plots, building plots, and redundant barns or farm buildings suitable for conversion in Northamptonshire.

How does an Option Agreement work when selling land in Northamptonshire?

An Option Agreement gives us the right to purchase your land at an agreed price once planning permission is secured. We fund and manage the planning process at our own risk. You receive a guaranteed sale price with no upfront cost or obligation.

Are there any fees to sell my land in Northamptonshire?

No. We are direct buyers and there are no agents' fees, no hidden costs, and no obligations. We deal with you directly throughout the entire process.
