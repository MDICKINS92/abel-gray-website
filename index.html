Investment, land acquisition and bespoke residential development, built on trust and integrity.

Abel Gray is a privately owned property development and investment company delivering residential developments, long term commercial investments and acquiring land with development potential. We maintain an active portfolio of residential and commercial assets, with projects spanning site acquisition, planning strategy, design led development and long term asset management. Every project is overseen directly by us, from initial appraisal through to planning, construction and completion.

What We Do

Abel Gray operates across three core areas, creating bespoke new build homes, investing in commercial property for long-term growth, and working with landowners to unlock development potential through responsible planning and delivery.

New Build Homes

We create high-quality family homes that are sensitive to their surroundings and built to stand the test of time. From initial design through to final finishes, every detail is considered carefully, resulting in homes that feel refined, practical and built with pride.

Commercial Investment

We acquire and hold commercial property across London and surrounding areas, focusing on well-located assets with strong fundamentals, sustainable income and long-term potential, including opportunities with scope for redevelopment. Each investment is actively overseen by us with a disciplined, long-term approach.

Land & Planning

We work directly with landowners to unlock the potential in land and redundant buildings. Whether agricultural land, brownfield sites or barns suitable for conversion, we secure sites through option agreements or outright purchase, take on planning risk and deliver the development through to completion.

Our Approach

01

Full Responsibility

We take responsibility for each stage of the process, from appraisal and acquisition through planning, funding and delivery, overseeing every project closely as a family-run business.

02

Transparency & Honesty

Whether you are selling land, discussing an investment opportunity or purchasing a home, we communicate clearly and set realistic expectations from the outset.

03

Planning Risk Taken by Us

We secure sites through option agreements or outright purchase and fund the planning process at our own risk. From appraisal and strategy through to securing consent and delivering the development, responsibility sits with us, providing landowners with clarity, certainty and a straightforward route to unlocking value.

04

Discretion & Trust

All discussions are handled confidentially and with respect. We approach every opportunity with care for the land, the local area and the people involved.
