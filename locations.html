Why Sell Your Land to Us

Abel Gray is a privately owned property development and investment company actively acquiring land across the Midlands and South East. We work directly with landowners, with no agents and no middlemen, to unlock value in land with development potential. We buy land with or without planning permission, promote land through the planning system at our own cost and risk, and can structure conditional purchases, Option Agreements or outright acquisitions depending on what suits the landowner best. We fund the entire planning and development process ourselves, from appraisal and planning strategy through to securing consent and completing the development. There are no fees, no obligations, and every enquiry is treated in complete confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions About Selling Land

Do I need planning permission before selling my land?

No. We buy land both with and without planning permission. We have an experienced planning team who assess the potential of every site and take on the full planning risk ourselves.

What types of land do you buy?

We buy agricultural land, farmland, paddocks, orchards, garden land, brownfield sites, infill plots, building plots, redundant barns, farm buildings and development sites across the Midlands and South East.

How much is my land worth?

Land value depends on location, size, access, planning potential, and local demand. We provide honest, realistic valuations and are happy to discuss your land's potential in a confidential, no-obligation conversation.

Are there any fees to sell my land?

No. We are direct buyers and there are no agents' fees, no hidden costs, and no obligations. We deal with you directly throughout the entire process.

How long does it take to sell land?

It depends on the route we agree. If we purchase outright, we can complete quickly using our own capital. If we proceed via an Option Agreement, we manage the planning process and complete upon securing planning permission.

Do you buy small plots of land?

Yes. We buy land of all sizes, from single building plots and garden land to larger agricultural sites. Every enquiry is treated with equal importance.
