Frequently Asked Questions: Selling Land in Buckinghamshire

Do you buy land without planning permission in Buckinghamshire?

Yes. We buy land with or without planning permission in Buckinghamshire. We assess every site on its development potential and manage the entire planning process ourselves at our own risk and cost.

What types of land do you buy in Buckinghamshire?

We buy agricultural land, paddocks, orchards, garden land, brownfield sites, infill plots, building plots, and redundant barns or farm buildings suitable for conversion in Buckinghamshire.

How does an Option Agreement work when selling land in Buckinghamshire?

An Option Agreement gives us the right to purchase your land at an agreed price once planning permission is secured. We fund and manage the planning process at our own risk. You receive a guaranteed sale price with no upfront cost or obligation.

Are there any fees to sell my land in Buckinghamshire?

No. We are direct buyers and there are no agents' fees, no hidden costs, and no obligations. We deal with you directly throughout the entire process.
