Quality New Build Homes

At Abel Gray, we create homes designed to stand the test of time. Every property is bespoke: site-acquired, design-led, and delivered through to completion by our own team. Our focus is on getting the detail right: considered layouts, quality specification, and construction that reflects real pride in the finished product.

Our homes are never rushed. At every stage, from initial site appraisal and design to construction and completion, we make considered decisions to ensure quality, longevity and a space you'll be proud to call home.

Quality of Workmanship at the Centre

Every Abel Gray home is constructed to the highest standards, using trusted trades and premium materials chosen for durability as well as appearance. From structure to final finishes, our team delivers craftsmanship that is immediately apparent in every detail.

Every property comes with a 10 year structural warranty, providing peace of mind and reassurance that your home has been built properly and with care. We build each property as if it were for our own family, ensuring longevity, comfort, and lasting style.

What Sets Our Homes Apart

01

Pride in Every Build

We never rush. Our homes are carefully crafted alongside trusted tradespeople who share our ethos. Every stage is managed in-house to ensure each element is thoughtfully executed, delivering stress-free, ready-to-move-in homes.

02

Premium Finish & Specification

We specify high-quality fixtures and fittings as standard. Kitchens feature NEFF and Bora appliances with quartz or granite worktops, while bathrooms are fitted with Villeroy & Boch sanitaryware and Hansgrohe fittings. Every choice balances functionality, durability, and enduring style.

03

Thoughtful Design

We design spaces for how they are lived in, not just how they look on paper. Layouts are practical, intuitive and enhance the natural flow of each home, with attention to sunlight, circulation, and comfort.

04

Local Character & Context

Every home is designed to complement its surroundings and reflect the local environment. Whether a single dwelling or a small collection, our homes combine quality, comfort, and enduring appeal.
